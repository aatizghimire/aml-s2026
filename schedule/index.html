    Schedule

    Here you will find the detailed weekly schedule and themes of the course. This proposed schedule is indicative and may be refined based on instructional needs and pacing.

    PART 1: MACHINE LEARNING CRASH COURSE

    Week 1: ML Fundamentals

    • Python for data science (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib)
    • Supervised learning algorithms
    • Feature engineering and selection
    • Model evaluation and hyperparameter tuning
    • Workshop: Build your first classifier

    Week 2: Deep Learning Foundations

    • Neural networks and backpropagation
    • PyTorch tensors and autograd
    • CNNs and transfer learning
    • Transformers and attention
    • Workshop: Train CNN on CIFAR-10

    PART 2: ADVERSARIAL ATTACKS

    Week 3: Open-Box Evasion

    • FGSM, PGD, Carlini & Wagner L2
    • Targeted vs untargeted attacks
    • Optimization for adversarial examples
    • Workshop: Implement CW attack

    Week 4: Closed-Box Evasion

    • SimBA (Simple Black-Box Attack)
    • HopSkipJump algorithm
    • Gradient estimation via sampling
    • Workshop: Build SimBA and HSJ

    Week 5: Model Extraction

    • API-based model stealing
    • Proof-Pudding (CVE-2019-20634)
    • AMSI classifier extraction
    • Workshop: Extract proxy model

    Week 6: Assessment Tools

    • ART (Adversarial Robustness Toolbox)
    • TextAttack framework
    • Alibi explainability
    • Workshop: Full model assessment

    Week 7: Model Inversion

    • Training data recovery
    • MIFace attack
    • Diffusion model extraction
    • Workshop: Invert models

    Week 8: Data Poisoning

    • Clean-label vs dirty-label
    • Witches' Brew gradient matching
    • Backdoor triggers
    • Workshop: Poison a classifier

    Week 9: LLM Security

    • Prompt injection techniques
    • LangChain exploitation (CVE-2023-29374)
    • Training data extraction
    • Workshop: Attack LLMs

    PART 3: ASSESSMENT

    Week 10: Final Assessment

    • 6 attack notebooks
    • 4 hours timed
    • 90/100 points to pass
    • Format: online
